RULES / INSTRUCTION

  • There are seven rows vertically and six rows horizontally.
  • The two colors of the checkers are red or black
  • First player drops one of their color checkers into one of the slots in the board. Then, players alternate turns dropping checkers into the board
  • The object of the game is to get four of your color checkers either vertically, horizontally, or diagonally.
